Teachers everywhere create video classes for their students. So, it feels logical for us to integrate interactive activities for these classes. E-squadron offers you a safe and enriched learning environment for all your students and an easy-to-use teacher dashboard that will save you time and effort but will guarantee excellent students progress measurement.

1000+ Online Activities

Assign and allow your students to discover while they learn about various topics.

Online Homework

Send interactive assignments for your students to work on in class or at home.

Games and Drills

Practice with games based on expressions of the Video exercises. Our range of learning games allows you to achieve the level of replay exposure needed for long-term memorization.

Reporting Tools

See your students’ results both: qualitatively or quantitatively for each activity in real-time

Critical thinking Activities

Promote your students with higher-order thinking skills with our graphic organizers and discussion activities tools. Make them think-develop and share their thoughts.
